=======================
Upgrade to Nextcloud 28
=======================

General
-------

info.xml
^^^^^^^^

Make sure your ``appinfo/info.xml`` allows for Nextcloud 28.

.. code-block:: xml

    <dependencies>
        <nextcloud min-version="25" max-version="28" />
    </dependencies>
