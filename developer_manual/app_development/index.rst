===============
App Development
===============

This section covers everything you need to build, test, and publish Nextcloud apps.

Nextcloud supports two development approaches:

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1

   php/index
   exapp/index
   release_notes/index

**PHP App Development** is the traditional approach. Your app runs inside the Nextcloud server, uses the OCP PHP API, and follows the standard app structure.

**ExApp Development** uses Docker containers. Your app runs as a separate service and can be written in any language.
