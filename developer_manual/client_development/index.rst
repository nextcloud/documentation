==================
Client Development
==================

This section covers building mobile apps, desktop apps, and third-party integrations that communicate with Nextcloud over the network.

Client developers primarily use the **OCS API** and **WebDAV API** (documented in :doc:`/api_reference/index`) for data access, and the **Login Flow** for authentication.
